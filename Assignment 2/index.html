About The Beats!

Speed Speech is an innovative company dedicated to transforming speech therapy for children through the power of AI and interactive technology. We specialize in selling a unique DJ-inspired speech therapy set, which includes an AI-powered headset and control board designed to make learning fun and effective. Our product combines cutting-edge speech recognition technology with engaging exercises and games, helping children develop their communication skills with confidence and enjoyment. At Speed Speech, we believe every child deserves a voice—and we’re here to help them find it!
